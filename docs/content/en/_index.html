Goployer handles the whole processes of application deployment with AWS Autoscaling, Load Balancer, and EC2 instances.
{{% blocks/lead color="primary" %}} Goployer controls the whole process of deploying instances with **AWS autoscaling group**. It creates new autoscaling group with new version,attaches it to the target group of ELB and terminates the previous autoscaling groups. {{% /blocks/lead %}} {{< blocks/testimonial-section >}}
